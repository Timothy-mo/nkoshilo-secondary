Who Can Apply?

Nkoshilo Secondary School welcomes all learners who are committed to academic excellence and discipline. We accept applications for Grades 8 to 12, depending on space availability.

Required Documents

  • Latest school report
  • Certified copy of learner’s birth certificate or ID
  • Parent/guardian’s certified ID copy
  • Proof of residence (not older than 3 months)
  • Transfer letter (for learners from other schools)

Important Dates

Applications open: 2nd June
Closing date: 30th November
Late applications will only be considered if space is still available.

How to Apply

Click the button below to access the online application form. Fill out all required fields and upload the necessary documents. You will receive a confirmation once your application is submitted.
