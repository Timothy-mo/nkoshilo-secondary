Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy outlines how Nkoshilo High School ("we", "our", or "the school") collects, uses, and protects your information when using our digital platforms.

1. Information Collection

We may collect basic information such as names, grades, and communication preferences. This data is gathered when learners or parents interact with our school platforms or forms.

2. Use of Information

Collected information is used strictly for academic and administrative purposes, such as sending updates, managing class schedules, or tracking academic progress.

3. Data Protection

We take reasonable precautions to protect all user data. We do not sell or share your information with external parties without consent, except where required by law.

4. Cookies

Our website may use basic cookies to enhance user experience and load preferences. You may disable cookies in your browser settings.

5. External Links

We may link to trusted educational or government websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external sites.

6. Updates

This policy may be updated from time to time. Major changes will be announced on our website or official communication platforms.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, please reach out to our school admin office or email nkoshilohs@gmail.com.
